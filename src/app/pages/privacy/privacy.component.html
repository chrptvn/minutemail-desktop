Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

MinuteMail is designed with privacy as our core principle. We provide temporary email addresses to protect your real email from spam, tracking, and unwanted communications. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal Information: We don't require registration, so we don't collect names, real email addresses, or personal details
  • Account Data: No usernames, passwords, or account profiles are created or stored
  • Payment Information: Our service is completely free, so no payment data is collected
  • Email Content Storage: Emails are automatically deleted when they expire

What We Temporarily Store

  • Temporary Email Addresses: Generated aliases are stored only for their active lifetime
  • Received Emails: Messages sent to your temporary address are stored temporarily and automatically deleted upon expiration
  • Session Data: Your current temporary email is stored in your browser's session storage (not on our servers)
  • Theme Preference: Your dark/light mode preference is stored locally in your browser

Automatic Data Deletion

All temporary email addresses and their associated messages are automatically deleted after expiration. We don't maintain any permanent records of:

  • Email content or attachments
  • Sender or recipient information
  • Usage patterns or access logs
  • IP addresses or location data

Technical Information

To provide our service, we may temporarily process:

  • Server Logs: Basic technical logs for service operation (automatically deleted)
  • Browser Data: Session storage and local storage for functionality (stays on your device)
  • Cookies: Only essential cookies for theme preferences and basic functionality

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Analytics: We use privacy-focused analytics (Plausible) that doesn't track individuals or use cookies
  • CDN: Content delivery for faster loading (no personal data shared)
  • Affiliate Links: Some privacy tool recommendations may include affiliate links (clearly marked)

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect the temporary data we process:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No permanent data storage reduces security risks

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or permanently store personal data:

  • No Data to Delete: All data is automatically deleted upon expiration
  • No Tracking: We don't track users across sessions or devices
  • Full Control: You control when to generate new addresses and when to stop using the service
  • Transparency: This policy explains exactly what we do and don't do with data

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy regulations. Our minimal data collection approach ensures compliance by design.

Service Limitations

Please note:

  • Temporary emails are not suitable for important communications
  • We cannot recover expired emails or addresses
  • The service is provided "as-is" without warranties
  • We reserve the right to block abusive usage

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, you can reach us through our website or create a temporary email address to contact us.
